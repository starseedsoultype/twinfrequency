StarSeedSoul is operated by Olesya Popova as an individual. We provide digital products and live events including soul origin readings, audio activations, relationship guides, workshops, and personal sessions.

Contact: timsocion@gmail.com
Instagram: @starseedsoul.type

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Through Gumroad (Payment Processor)

All purchases are processed by Gumroad Inc. When you make a purchase, Gumroad collects and processes your payment information, email address, and transaction data. We receive your email address from Gumroad in order to deliver your purchased product, provide customer support, and send you updates about new StarSeedSoul products and events.

You may opt out of these emails at any time by contacting us at timsocion@gmail.com.

We do not collect or store your payment card details. These are handled entirely by Gumroad.

Gumroad's Privacy Policy applies to all payment transactions: gumroad.com/privacy

2.2 Through Telegram Bot

When you interact with our Telegram bot (@SeedSoulTest_bot) to receive a free PDF, your Telegram username is visible to us through the Telegram platform. We do not store or process this data beyond what is necessary to deliver the requested file.

Telegram's Privacy Policy applies: telegram.org/privacy

2.3 Through Calendly (Session Booking)

When you book a personal reading session, scheduling is handled through Calendly. Calendly collects your name, email address, and time zone in order to facilitate the booking. We receive this information to confirm and conduct your session.

Calendly's Privacy Policy applies: calendly.com/pages/privacy

2.4 Through Event Registration Forms

When you register for a live workshop or event, we collect your name and email address through the registration form. This information is used solely to confirm your registration and communicate event details.

2.5 Website

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts on our website. We do not actively collect or process any personal data through the website itself. GitHub Pages may retain standard server logs as part of their hosting infrastructure, governed by their own privacy policy.

2.6 Direct Communication

If you contact us by email, Instagram, or any other channel, we retain that correspondence in order to respond to you and provide support.

We use the information we receive only for the following purposes:
